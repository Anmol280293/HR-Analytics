# HR Analytics Dashboard


---

# Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is a business intelligence project developed using **Power BI** to analyze employee data and generate insights related to **employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and departmental performance**.

The goal of this project is to help organizations **monitor workforce trends, understand attrition patterns, and support data-driven HR decisions**.

This dashboard provides an **interactive visualization interface** that allows HR professionals to quickly identify problem areas and improve workforce management strategies.

---

# Business Objective

Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational performance. The objective of this project is to analyze HR data and answer important business questions such as:

* What is the current employee attrition rate?
* Which departments experience the highest attrition?
* How does salary vary across job roles?
* What are the demographic patterns of employees?
* How does employee performance vary across departments?

By answering these questions, organizations can **improve retention strategies and optimize HR policies**.

# Dashboard Features

## 1 Employee Overview

The dashboard provides a quick overview of the workforce through key metrics including:

* Total employees
* Active employees
* Attrition rate
* Average employee age

These metrics provide a **high-level summary of the organization's workforce**.

---

## 2 Attrition Analysis

The dashboard analyzes employee attrition across different departments:

Departments included:

* Research & Development
* Sales
* Human Resources

This analysis helps identify departments with **higher turnover rates**, allowing management to take targeted actions.

---

## 3 Workforce Demographics

Employee data is segmented by:

* Age groups
* Gender
* Marital status
* Education level

Age groups include:

* Under 25
* 25 – 34
* 35 – 44
* 45 – 54
* Over 55

The **25–44 age range** represents the majority of the workforce.

---

## 4 Education Background Analysis

The dashboard analyzes employee distribution across education fields such as:

* Life Sciences
* Medical
* Marketing
* Technical Degree
* Human Resources
* Other

This helps organizations understand **employee academic backgrounds and hiring trends**.

---

## 5 Salary Analysis

The project includes an analysis of **average monthly income by job role**, including:

* Manager
* Research Director
* Manufacturing Director
* Sales Executive
* Laboratory Technician
* Sales Representative

This insight helps HR teams **evaluate compensation structures** across different roles.

---

## 6 Performance Rating Analysis

Employee performance ratings are analyzed across job roles to understand how performance varies within the organization.

Performance ratings range from **1 to 4**, helping identify high-performing roles and departments.

---

# Key Insights

From the analysis, several insights were discovered:

* The **attrition rate is 16.1%**, indicating potential retention challenges.
* Most employees belong to **Life Sciences and Medical education fields**.
* The **25–34 and 35–44 age groups** dominate the workforce.
* Higher salary levels are observed in **managerial and director-level positions**.
* Research & Development and Sales departments have the largest workforce.
